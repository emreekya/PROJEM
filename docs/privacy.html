Privacy Policy

MONEYPRINTER — Last updated: June 3, 2025

1. Introduction

MONEYPRINTER ("we", "our", or "the Application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered video creation service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you connect social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram), we receive OAuth access tokens to publish content on your behalf.
  • Content Data: Video topics, scripts, and generated content created through the Application.
  • Usage Data: Application usage logs for debugging and improving the service.
  • API Credentials: API keys for third-party services, stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and publish videos to your connected social media accounts
  • To authenticate with TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram APIs on your behalf
  • To improve the Application's AI content generation capabilities
  • To provide technical support

4. TikTok API Data

When you connect your TikTok account, we access the following data through TikTok's official API:

  • user.info.basic: Your TikTok username and profile information
  • video.publish: Permission to publish videos to your TikTok account
  • video.upload: Permission to upload video files to TikTok

We do not sell, share, or use your TikTok account data for any purpose other than publishing content you explicitly create through the Application.

5. Data Storage

All data, including OAuth tokens and API credentials, are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any remote servers that store your personal data or credentials.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to operate the Application (e.g., sending data to TikTok/YouTube APIs to publish your content).

7. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored locally until you disconnect your accounts or delete the Application. Generated video content is stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All API communications use HTTPS encryption. Access tokens are stored locally and are not transmitted to our servers.

9. Your Rights

You may revoke access to your social media accounts at any time through the respective platform settings (TikTok Settings → Manage Apps, YouTube → Google Account Permissions). You can delete all local data by uninstalling the Application.

10. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
